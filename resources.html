Scheduling & Logistics

Untangle a week of camps, practices, and drivers into one plan Claude keeps straight for you.

"Here's this week's activities for my 6 kids: [list]. Help me build a driver schedule and flag any conflicts."

🍽️

Meal Planning

Plan around picky eaters and a real grocery budget, not a Pinterest fantasy.

"Plan 4 weeknight dinners using mostly Trader Joe's/Costco staples. Picky eaters: [list what they'll actually eat]."

✉️

Communication

Emails to teachers, coaches, and camps — drafted in your voice, not a robot's.

"Draft a short, friendly email to my daughter's coach asking about the schedule change. Keep it casual."

🧾

Household Ops

Babysitter hours, shared calendars, and the admin nobody wants to own.

"Here are this week's sitter hours: [list]. Total them and draft the Venmo request."

Kid Admin

Permission slips, camp forms, and appointment juggling, sorted in one pass.

"Here's this month's forms and due dates: [list]. Put them in order and remind me what's due this week."
