The mom behind the mic

Hi, I'm Allison.

I have six kids. I didn't come to AI because I'm a tech person — I came to it because the mental load had nowhere left to go.

Between camp forms, PT, swim, hair appointments, cheer, a sitter schedule, and a calendar that never matched anyone else's, I hit the wall most moms know: too much in my head, no room for anything else.

My husband Bo told me to just try asking Claude — not to code anything, just to talk to it like a very patient assistant who never tires of my run-on sentences at 11pm. It changed how I run the house, and I couldn't stop telling people.

Moms Who Claude is that same conversation, opened up — a prompt library, courses, and yes, a podcast. No tech background, no jargon, no pretending any of this is glamorous. Just real prompts for real chaos, tested on my own six kids, Bo, and our sitters before they ever go live.

Everything here gets tested on:

6 kids 1 husband (Bo) 2 regular sitters ∞ camp forms Trader Joe's & duct tape

"I didn't want to learn AI. I wanted my brain back."
