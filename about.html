The mom behind the mic

Hi, I'm Allison.

I have six kids. I didn't come to AI because I'm a tech person — I came to it because nothing else was working.

Between camp forms, PT, swim, hair appointments, cheer, a babysitter schedule, and a calendar that somehow never matched anyone else's, I hit the point most moms hit: too much in my head, and no room left for anything else.

Bo, my husband, told me to just try asking Claude. Not to code anything — just to talk to it like a very patient assistant who never gets tired of my run-on sentences at 11pm. It changed how I run my house, and I couldn't stop telling people about it.

Moms Who Claude is that same conversation, opened up — a prompt library, courses, and yes, a podcast. No tech background required, no jargon, no pretending any of this is glamorous. Just real prompts for real chaos, tested on my own six kids, my husband Bo, and our regular sitters, before they ever go live.
